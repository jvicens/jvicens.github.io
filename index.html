Julián Vicens

Engineer and Scientist

Researcher in social complex systems, designing technological systems, performing public and collective experiments and delving into social data to understand behavioural patterns and address social issues. My study focuses on introducing public participation in the research process as a force of social transformation in the boundaries/intersection between science, technology, arts and activism. My work bridges the fields of complex systems, human-computer interaction, citizen science, computational social science, social experimentation, game theory, data science, and others.

Currently in OpenSystems, Complexity Lab Barcelona and University of Barcelona Institute of Complex Systems.

About Me

Born and raised in Mallorca. I am a Telecommunications Engineer and I hold a MSc on Telecommunications Networks by Universitat Ramon Llull (Barcelona, Spain), where I performed theses focused on acoustics, music technologies and natural user interfaces. After some years developing mobile applications for the private sector, I started my PhD within the Industrial Doctorate program, by which I combined the design and development of cultural products for a private company with research at the Department of Computer Science and Mathematics and the Department of Pedagogy in Universitat Rovira i Virgili (Tarragona, Spain). I developed and defended my PhD thesis entitled «Human Behavior Experimentation and Participation in Scientific Activities in the Wild» under the supervision of Prof. Mercè Gisbert and Dr. Jordi Duch. During my PhD, I was a visiting researcher at Northwestern University (Evanston, Illinois) in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science and the Segal Design Institute (Dr. Haoqi Zhang) and the Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering (Prof. Luis A. Amaral). Currently, I am working at the Department of Condensed Matter Physics of Universitat de Barcelona as a part of the research group OpenSystems with Dr. Josep Perelló, as well as Complexity Lab Barcelona and UBICS (Universitat de Barcelona Institute of Complex Systems).
