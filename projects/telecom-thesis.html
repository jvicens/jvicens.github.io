ENGINEERING THESIS

Tridimensional sound environment with a natural user interface

Engineering thesis that presents a tridimensional sound experience in real-time using a natural user interface and TUIO protocols. The interface reacts to the fiducials positions and codification creating a natural sound environment.

September, 2011
